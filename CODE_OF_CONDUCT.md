# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Everyone is welcome here. You are free to explore, contribute, and share your ideas in this project. We believe in openness, creativity, and collaboration.  

## Respect and Responsibility

Freedom comes with respect. Please:

* Be kind and considerate to others
* Respect differing opinions and experiences
* Avoid harassment, personal attacks, or sharing private information
* Focus on constructive contributions that help the community grow

## Reporting Issues

If you encounter behavior that is harmful or inappropriate, you can contact the project maintainer at **isherezahin@gmail.com**. All reports will be handled respectfully and privately.  

## Enforcement

Persistent or serious violations may result in restricted access or removal from the community to protect a safe and open environment for everyone.
